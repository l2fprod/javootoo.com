LipstikLF

Lipstik look and feel is intended to be a Java (tm) Swing counterpart of marvellous Kde theme engine called Lipstik

Despite many Swing LookAndFeels none of them suits well with native Kde apps decorated by Lipstik engine. This one makes KDE user's life a bit sweeter imitating Lipstik look. Although it's not exactly (pixel-to-pixel) the same as original, it should give you a nice impression :)
